Fried Chicken

Fried chicken is undoubtly the best chicken dish period. I don't even want to argue with this

Ingredients

  • 250 Grams Chicken
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup all purpost flour
  • 2 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt as required

Steps

  1. Prepare all the Ingredients
  2. Dry the wet
  3. Wet the dry
  4. Fry the dry
  5. Dry the wet with sauce
