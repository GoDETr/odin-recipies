Butter Chicken

Butter chicken is undoubtly the second best chicken dish or the best chicken curry period

Ingredients

  • 250 Grams Chicken
  • 1/4 cup full fat yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ginger garlic paste
  • 2 teaspoon olive oil
  • Salt as required

Steps

  1. Prepare all the Ingredients
  2. Cut the chicken
  3. Fry onion and tomatoes
  4. Grind it
  5. Fry the chicken
  6. Assemble it
